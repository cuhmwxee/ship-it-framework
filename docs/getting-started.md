# Getting Started

## Who is this for?

- Solo developers
- Small teams
- Large organizations
- AI-assisted development

---

## The only workflow

Input

↓

Development

↓

Validation

↓

Ship

---

## Step 1

Pick one change.

Do not optimize the workflow.

Just pick one change.

---

## Step 2

Develop it.

Use whatever tools you already use.

---

## Step 3

Validate it independently.

Independent does not necessarily mean another person.

It means the validation is capable of proving the work correct.

---

## Step 4

Ship.

Repeat.

---

## Common first mistakes

Trying to redesign the workflow.

Trying to introduce roles.

Trying to replace Scrum or Kanban.

Trying to automate everything first.

---

## You're already using Ship It! if...

- every change has an Input
- every change is developed
- every change is validated
- every validated change can be shipped
